 The key cotton diseases mentioned in the context include Alternaria leaf spot (ALS) caused by Alternaria alternata and other minor species, Black root rot, Boll rot, tight lock and seed rot, Cotton bunchy top, Fusarium wilt, Nematodes, Ramularia leaf spot, Reoccurring wilt, Seedling diseases, Tobacco streak virus (TSV), and Verticillium wilt. Other less common diseases include bacterial blight, bacterial stunt, post-harvest rot, sudden wilt, and tropical rust.
