 The key insect pests for cotton cultivation in Australia, as outlined in the given context, include Helicoverpa (Bollworm), aphids, mealybugs, spider mites, Mirids, Jassids (Thrips), and two-spotted mites. Understanding their behavior, damage potential, preventative measures, and suitable control techniques is essential for effective insect management in cotton farming.
