The major nutrients taken up by cotton include both macronutrients and micronutrients. The primary macronutrients are:1. **Nitrogen (N)**2. **Phosphorus (P)**3. **Potassium (K)**4. **Calcium (Ca)**5. **Magnesium (Mg)**6. **Sulfur (S)**The micronutrients essential for cotton growth include:1. **Iron (Fe)**2. **Manganese (Mn)**3. **Zinc (Zn)**4. **Copper (Cu)**5. **Boron (B)**6. **Molybdenum (Mo)**7. **Chlorine (Cl)**These nutrients are crucial for various physiological and biochemical processes within the cotton plant, including protein synthesis, energy transfer, and structural development.
