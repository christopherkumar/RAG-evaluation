 The 2018 edition of NUTRIpak includes several updates compared to earlier versions. Some of these updates are:

1. Higher average yields: The 2018 edition takes into account the higher average yields now being achieved by Australian cotton growers. This means that the nutrient requirements and exports have been updated accordingly.

2. New research findings: The 2018 edition includes new research findings in the field of cotton nutrition, such as advances in nutrient uptake and use efficiency, as well as new insights into the role of soil health and biology in supporting optimal cotton growth and productivity.

3. Improved nutrient management recommendations: Based on the latest research findings and updated nutrient requirements, the 2018 edition provides improved nutrient management recommendations to help advisers and growers optimize their cotton nutrition programs and maximize their yields and profits while minimizing their environmental footprint.
