 The key insect pests can vary depending on the specific crop and region. However, some common key insect pests include aphids, armyworm (Spodoptera species), helicoverpa, mirids, mites, soil and establishment pests, stink bugs and stainers, thrips, whitefly, and other pests. It is important to consult with local agricultural extension services or crop protection specialists for specific information on key insect pests and effective control strategies in your area.
