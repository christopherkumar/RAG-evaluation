The cotton weeds that have reported glyphosate resistance include:

Windmill grass (Chloris truncata)
Fleabane (Conyza bonariensis)
Feathertop Rhodes grass (Chloris virgata)
Awnless barnyard grass (Echinochloa colona)
Sowthistle (Sonchus oleraceus)
Liverseed grass (Urochloa panicoides)
Annual ryegrass (Lolium rigidum)
Additionally, there are concerns about emerging resistance in other weed species such as tall fleabane and some grasses​​.
